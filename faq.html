Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

Do I need to have previous knowledge?

Some courses allow you to learn from scratch, others require prior basic knowledge, we advise you to read the programmes. Our teachers will be available to answer any questions you may have, so that you will be prepared to get the most out of our courses.

Do I need to have my own computer?

Yes, we do not provide computers. It is the student’s responsibility to use their own computer with internet access.

What do we offer?

With our courses and programs you will be able to learn technical knowledge and the methodology to work professionally integrating techniques, tools and technologies.

How is our study methodology?

The courses are dictated 100% live online and the classes are recorded. They include downloadable resources, videos, exam simulators and support with the teaching team so that you can clear all your doubts.

Do you award certificates at the end of the course?

Yes, we provide a digital certificate.

How do I enroll in a course?

To join any course you must register and pay for it. If a course has already started, you can still register, watch past classes that are recorded and take the next online and live classes

Which payment methods are available?

You can pay with PayPal, Visa, MasterCard, Discover, American Express, Payoneer and Bitcoin. We are working to incorporate new payment methods. For more information, please contact us.
