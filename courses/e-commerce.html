E-Commerce training course

Who is this course for?

This course is aimed at any person interested in training and acquiring technological tools to sell products and services online, either within a marketplace or creating their own store.

At the end of this course you will be able to:

  • Develop skills to create and manage an e-commerce project
  • Know the main technological platforms for online sales
  • Analyze platforms and channels to set up an e-commerce site
  • Analyze payment methods, logistics and promotion channels
  • Analyze indicators to perform site analytics

Modality:

  • 100% live online
  • Digital course certificate

Duration:

  • 22 classes of 3 hours each
  • Ask for available days and times contact us

Included resources:

  • Downloadable study material
  • Downloadable practice material

Study Program:

  • Module 1: Introduction to e-commerce
  • Module 2: Online sales processes
  • Module 3: Virtual payment methods
  • Module 4: Logistics and distribution for e-Commerce
  • Module 5: Technological platforms
  • Module 6: Data and metrics (KPI)
  • Module 7: Commercial strategies
  • Module 8: Digital communication
