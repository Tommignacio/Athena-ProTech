Project Management course

Who is this course for?

This course is oriented to any person interested in obtaining knowledge about methodologies and tools to plan and organize the tasks of the projects you are working on, in order to accomplish the proposed objectives in time and form.

At the end of this course you will be able to:

  • Obtain a comprehensive view of project management
  • Manage communication in order to be effective
  • Lead a multidisciplinary team in an effective and efficient way
  • Understand methodologies and tools to plan and organize tasks
  • Know the fundamentals to manage digital projects
  • Optimize the interaction with different areas of an organization
  • Understand risk management

Modality:

  • 100% live online
  • Digital course certificate

Duration:

  • 22 classes of 2 hours each
  • Ask for available days and times contact us

Included resources:

  • Downloadable study material
  • Downloadable practice material

Study Program:

  • Module 1: Introduction to project management
  • Module 2: Communication fundamentals
  • Module 3: One shot digital projects
  • Module 4: Digital projects by objectives and always-on
  • Module 5: Planning and management of budget
  • Module 6: Change, quality and purchases management
  • Module 7: Risks management
  • Module 8: Slack and Google Workspace
